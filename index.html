CESSY  |   MUSIC

Ces Sy is a singer, songwriter, and musician living in the Caribbean Island of Aruba. She performs versions of songs from the classics up to today's generation of music. Her genre is an integration of Contemporary Music, Alternative and Pop Rock. Over the years, she has developed a powerful vocal style which gives her performance a unique feel. That's passion in every performance! Catch her in one of her live performances in the island.
